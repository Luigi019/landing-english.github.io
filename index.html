Project's Resume

With the purpose of putting into practice the cognitive tools for the motivation of ideas and promote the construction of knowledge for the improvement of an eventuality in the community, which arise as consequences of the needs to be adapted to the demanding changes demanded by the technological movement. All this was applied in the public entity "Inmobiliaria Nacional, S.A." for the project management of PNFI's IV path. This evaluation allowed auditing the system in terms of the quality of the software, database, physical and logical security, as well as the infrastructure that supports it, in order to be taken into account to incorporate improvements and in turn provide solutions that allow giving continuity to the work that is done through its use, the citizen attention system was developed in order to promote the ease of handling complaints, claims and suggestions from the beneficiaries of the great housing mission Venezuela.

Software

For the evaluation of the system of attention to the citizen in the national real estate regarding the internal quality of the software, the study of six (06) particularities was formalized:
Functionality, Reliability, Usability, Efficiency, Maintainability, Portability.
These particularities, subdivided into properties, result in a result that complies with ISO/IEC 9126-1 standards (Functionality, Reliability, Usability, Efficiency, Maintainability, Portability).

Database

Name of the Database: inmo_sac
Purpose of the Database: It is oriented to the automation of the processes concerning the management of complaints or claims from citizens, where Inmobiliaria Nacional acts receiving them, and managing them.
Database Manager: PostgreSQL
Database manager version: V12.7.2

Networks

Inmobiliaria Nacional S.A. has a star type topology that consists of a central switch where the workstations that distribute the services to the whole company are connected, the community has switches by floor that are segmented and the workstations are connected by department.
Networks / Sub-networks
The community has only one network.
Fixed
The national real estate company has 1 Metro Ethernet service network, supplied by CANTV with an 8 Mbps link.
Wireless
The community has a conventional home wifi router.

Risks

There is no procedures manual or documentation for reporting incidents.
The data center does not have the right temperature.
Shared folders and other confidential information can be found by a visitor.

Improvement proposals

In the management of computer networks it is essential to reduce connection and communication failures, in this process the current processes that the community has are identified and analyzed and to verify if it is adequate according to the established standards, thus helping to take preventive or corrective actions, as the case may be. The evaluation was based on the ISO/IEC 27033-1 and EIA/TIA 568 standards, which were in charge of standardizing the networks in all their aspects, and also goes hand in hand with ISO 27002 standards, which set out the quality that must be achieved. In order to comply with these standards, the following proposals were deployed: Development of manuals, standards and policies necessary to deal with network failures. To comply with control 11.2.3, it is necessary to repair existing air conditioners or purchase new equipment to avoid problems.
